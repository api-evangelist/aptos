Testing Move smart contracts on Aptos has always been tricky, as there were no easy ways to simulate transaction-based workflows with isolation and repeatability, let alone fork real network state for realistic testing. Until now. Today, we’re releasing Forklift , a Move smart contract testing and scripting framework for Aptos.
