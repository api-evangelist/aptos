Move in 2025: Building a Modern Smart Contract Language In 2025, Move continued to push the bar for being the most expressive, performant, and safe smart contract language. The value of Move was always easy to perceive at the language surface: you could represent assets and on-chain state directly, and the language enforced safety rails around how the assets can be created, moved, and destroyed. In 2025, the capabilities improved across the stack.
